Learn Iraqi Arabic

A source for learning this beautiful dialect of the Arabic language for free.

Examples:

How are you? (m.) = شلونك؟

shlonek

How are you? (f.) = شلونچ؟

shlonich

Good morning = صباح الخير

sabah el khair

Good evening = مساء الخير

masaa el khair

Good night (m.) = تصبح على خير

tissbah 'ela khair

Good night (f.) = تصبحين على خير

tissbaheen 'ela khair

Good night (plural) = تصبحون على خير

tissbahun 'ela khair

Thanks = شكراً

shukren

Thank you (m.) = اشكرك

ashkurek

Thank you (f.) = اشكرچ

ashkurich

Do you have ___? (m.) = عندك ___؟

endak

Do you have ___? (f.) = عندچ ___؟

endich

How much is this? = هاي شگد سعرها؟

haay shgad si'irha

How much is this? (another way of saying it) = هاي بيش؟

haay besh

How old are you? (m.) = شگد عمرك؟

shgad umrak

How old are you? (f.) = شگد عمرچ؟

shgad umrech

Where are you from? (m.) = انت منین؟

enta mnen

Where are you from? (f.) = انتي منين؟

enti mnen
