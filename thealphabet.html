The alphabet - الابجدية العربية


أ ب ت ث ج ح خ د ذ ر ز س ش ص ض ط ظ ع غ ف ق ك گ ل م ن ه و ي ى چ

Alef Baa Taa Thaa Jeem hhaa khaa daal dhaal raa zaay seen sheen dhaad taa dhaa ayn ghayn faa qaf kaf gaf lam meem noon haa waw yaa alef che

A B T TH J H KH D TH R Z S SH S DH T DH 'AA GH F Q G L M N H W/U/O Y/I A CH

Note: Arabic is written from right to left.

Reading exercises

مرحبا
hello
(marhaba)


شلونك
how are you (m.)
(shlonek)


اهلا و سهلا
welcome
(ahlan wa sahlan)


الله اكبر
allah is great
(allahu akbar)


صدگ؟
really?
(sudug)


هواية
a lot
(huwaye)


هذا
this
(hatha)


ذاك
that
(thak)


شلون الاحوال؟
how are things?
(shlon el ahhwal)

Harakat (Vowel marks)

َ = فتحة (fathha)


ُ = ضمة (dhama)


ِ = كسرة (kasra)


ّ = شدة = (shadda)


the fatha makes an "a" sound, the dhama makes a "w/u" sound, the kasra makes a "y/i" sound, the shadda duplicates the letter


دَ = da


دُ = du


دِ = di


دّ = dd


there are more harakat but these are the most common ones. I will give additional reading exercises.


دادا = dada


ديدي = didi


دَق = daq
